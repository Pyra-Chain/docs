# Connect Wallet

## Access Pyra Chain with Your Solana Wallet

Connect your existing **Solana wallet** to Pyra Chain via **Wallet Connect**. No new seed phrase needed - use your current Solana address to access the full Pyra Chain ecosystem: DEX, bridge, launchpad, private transfers, and more.

- **Method**: Wallet Connect (Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, etc.)
- **Network**: Your Solana address works on Pyra Chain
- **Signing**: All transactions signed through your Solana wallet
- **Interface**: Full functionality inside [pyrachain.io](https://pyrachain.io)

---

## How It Works

1. **Connect**: Link your Solana wallet via Wallet Connect
2. **Access**: Your address is now active on Pyra Chain
3. **Interact**: Send tokens, swap, bridge - all within pyrachain.io
4. **Sign**: Transaction requests sent to your Solana wallet for approval

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Go to Pyra Chain
Open the main portal.

**Action**: Visit [pyrachain.io](https://pyrachain.io)

---

### Step 2: Click Connect Wallet
Initiate the connection process.

**Action**:
1. Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the top-right header
2. Wallet Connect modal opens

---

### Step 3: Select Your Wallet
Choose your Solana wallet provider.

**Supported Wallets**:
- **Phantom** (recommended)
- **Solflare**
- **Backpack**
- **Other Wallet Connect compatible wallets**

**Action**:
1. Select your wallet from the list
2. Your wallet app opens (or browser extension activates)
3. Approve the connection request in your wallet

---

### Step 4: Wallet Connected!
You now have full access to Pyra Chain.

**What You Can Do**:
- View your $PYRA balance on Pyra Chain
- Send and receive tokens
- Swap on DEX
- Bridge between Solana and Pyra Chain
- Create and trade tokens on Launchpad
- Use Private Transfers
- Vote on Petitions

---

## Signing Transactions

When you perform actions on Pyra Chain (swap, send, bridge, etc.):

1. **Action**: You initiate a transaction on pyrachain.io
2. **Request**: Transaction details sent to your Solana wallet
3. **Review**: Check the transaction in your wallet
4. **Approve**: Sign the transaction
5. **Confirmed**: Transaction executes on Pyra Chain

> **Note**: All signing happens in your Solana wallet - Pyra Chain never has access to your private keys.

---

## Managing Your Wallet

### View Balance
Your $PYRA and token balances are displayed in the wallet section at [pyrachain.io/wallet](https://pyrachain.io/wallet).

### Send Tokens
1. Go to [pyrachain.io/wallet](https://pyrachain.io/wallet)
2. Click **"Send"**
3. Enter recipient address and amount
4. Approve transaction in your Solana wallet

### Receive Tokens
Share your wallet address (same as your Solana address) to receive $PYRA or tokens on Pyra Chain.

### Disconnect
1. Click your wallet address in the header
2. Select **"Disconnect"**
3. Your session ends (funds remain safe in your wallet)

## Security

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| **No New Keys** | Use your existing Solana wallet - no new seed phrase |
| **Non-Custodial** | Pyra Chain never stores your private keys |
| **Wallet Signing** | Every transaction requires your wallet approval |
| **Session Only** | Connection is temporary - disconnect anytime |

---

## Tips

- **Use Trusted Wallets**: Phantom, Solflare, Backpack are recommended
- **Verify Site**: Always check you're on [pyrachain.io](https://pyrachain.io)
- **Review Transactions**: Check details before signing
- **Disconnect When Done**: End session on shared devices
