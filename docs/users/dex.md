# DEX

## Overview

The **Pyra Chain DEX** at [pyrachain.io/dex](https://pyrachain.io/dex) is a **high-performance decentralized exchange** built for **instant swaps** of **Pyra Chain-native tokens** using **$PYRA** as the base pair. It features **deep liquidity**, **low fees**, and **real-time analytics** - all powered by the **41K TPS** Solana-based infrastructure.

---

## Process Overview

1. **Browse Pools** → View top pairs by volume, liquidity, or market cap.
2. **Connect Wallet** → Connect your Solana wallet with $PYRA balance.
3. **Select Pair & Amount** → Choose "From" and "To" tokens.
4. **Review & Confirm** → Approve transaction in your wallet.

**Fees**: **burned synchronously** across **Solana & Pyra Chain**.

---

## Step 1: Access DEX & Browse Pools
Go to the DEX and explore available trading pairs.

**Action**:
1. Visit [pyrachain.io/dex](https://pyrachain.io/dex)
2. View **top stats**: Total Market Cap, Liquidity, 24h Volume, Active Pools
3. Use **sort dropdown**:
   - Newest first
   - Highest Volume (recommended)
   - Highest Liquidity
   - Highest Market Cap
4. Browse table:
   - **Pool**: e.g., `PYRA/TOKEN` with icons and social links
   - **Market Cap**: e.g., **$42K**
   - **Liquidity**: e.g., **$12.5K**
   - **Volume 24h**: e.g., **$20K**
   - **Action**: **SWAP** button

---

## Step 2: Connect Wallet
Ensure your wallet is connected on the **Pyra Chain network**.

**Action**:
1. Click **"Connect Wallet"** (top-right)
2. Confirm connection

> **Warning**: You must have **$PYRA on Pyra Chain** to swap. [Bridge first](https://pyrachain.io/bridge) if needed.

---

## Step 3: Open Swap Modal
Click **SWAP** on any pool to open the trade interface.

**Action**:
1. Click **SWAP** on a pool
2. Modal opens:
   - **Contract**: Token contract address
   - **Social Icons**: Telegram, X, Website
   - **From** → `PYRA` (default)
   - **To** → Selected token

---

## Step 4: Enter Amount & Review
Input the swap amount and verify details.

**Action**:
1. In **From**: Enter amount of **$PYRA** (e.g., 1.0)
   - Balance shown: e.g., `0.000`
2. **To** field auto-fills with estimated output
3. Review:
   - **Exchange Rate**: e.g., `1 PYRA = X TOKEN`
   - **Price Impact**: e.g., `0%` (low due to deep liquidity)

---

## Step 5: Confirm & Swap
Execute the transaction.

**Action**:
1. Click **SWAP** (green-blue gradient button)
2. Approve transaction in your Solana wallet
3. Wait ~1 second → **Swap Complete!**
4. View receipt in [explorer.pyrachain.io](https://explorer.pyrachain.io)
