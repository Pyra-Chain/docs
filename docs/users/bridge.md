# Cross-Chain Bridge

## Transfer $PYRA Between Solana and Pyra Chain

The **Pyra Chain Bridge** enables **seamless, secure, and fast** transfers of **$PYRA** between **Solana** and **Pyra Chain** networks - maintaining a **1:1 peg** with **~30-second average confirmation**.

> - **Average Transfer Time**: **~30 sec**
> - **Bridge Fee**: **1 $PYRA** *(+ $0.50 if no $PYRA on Solana)*

---

## How to Use the Bridge

### Step 1: Connect Your Solana Wallet
Connect your wallet to access the bridge.

**Action**:
1. Visit [pyrachain.io/bridge](https://pyrachain.io/bridge)
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the top-right header
3. Select your wallet (Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, etc.)
4. Approve the connection in your wallet

---

### Step 2: Select Direction & Amount
Choose whether to bridge **Solana → Pyra Chain** or **Pyra Chain → Solana**, then enter the amount.

**Action**:
1. Select **From**: `Solana` or `Pyra Chain`
2. Select **To**: `Pyra Chain` or `Solana`
3. Enter amount (minimum: **10,000 $PYRA**)
4. Check balance and fees

---

### Step 3: Confirm & Bridge
Review the transaction details and execute the transfer.

**Fees Breakdown**:
- **Base Fee**: 1 $PYRA
- **Extra Fee** (if no $PYRA on Solana): $0.50 (converted to $PYRA at current rate)

**Action**:
1. Review: Amount, Direction, Fees
2. Click **"Bridge"**
3. Approve transaction in your Solana wallet
4. Wait ~30 seconds → **Completed!**
