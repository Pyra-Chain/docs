# Manage Socials

## Update Token Social Links

After creating your token on Pyra Chain, you can easily **update social links** (Website, Telegram, Twitter/X) to boost visibility and community engagement. These links appear on the token's page in the launchpad, DEX, and explorer, helping users connect with your project.

- **Access**: Via Creator Dashboard - no extra fees
- **Network**: Pyra Chain (wallet connected)
- **Time**: < 15 seconds
- **Changes**: Live instantly - no approval needed

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Access Creator Dashboard
Open the creator section to view your tokens.

**Action**:
1. Visit [pyrachain.io/launch](https://pyrachain.io/launch) or [pyrachain.io](https://pyrachain.io)
2. Click your **wallet address** in the top-right corner
3. Or go directly to [pyrachain.io/creator](https://pyrachain.io/creator)
4. Connect wallet if prompted

The dashboard shows:
- **Total Fees**: (across all tokens)
- **Unclaimed Fees**: Available to claim
- **Created Tokens**: Cards for each token

---

### Step 2: Select Token & Manage Socials
Choose a token and open its management modal.

**Action**:
1. Scroll to **Created Tokens** section
2. Find your token card with:
   - Logo, name, social icons
   - **Total Fees** and **Unclaimed Fees** in $PYRA
   - Buttons: **Claim Fees** (green), **Manage Socials** (purple)
3. Click **Manage Socials**

---

### Step 3: Update Social Links
Edit the optional social fields in the modal.

**Action**:
1. Modal opens with current details:
   - **Contract Address**: (read-only)
   - **Social Icons**: Telegram, X, Website
2. Update fields:
   - **Website (Optional)**: e.g., `https://pyrachain.io/`
   - **Telegram (Optional)**: e.g., `https://t.me/PyraChannel`
   - **Twitter (Optional)**: e.g., `https://x.com/pyrachain`

---

### Step 4: Save Changes
Confirm and apply updates.

**Action**:
1. Review entries
2. Click **Save Socials** (purple gradient button)
3. Approve transaction in your Solana wallet (minimal gas fee)
4. Wait ~5 seconds â†’ **Changes Live!**

Updates appear immediately on:
- Token page in [pyrachain.io/launch](https://pyrachain.io/launch)
- Pools in [pyrachain.io/dex](https://pyrachain.io/dex)
- Listings in [explorer.pyrachain.io](https://explorer.pyrachain.io)

---

## Tips & Security

- **Verify URL**: Always access via [pyrachain.io/creator](https://pyrachain.io/creator)
- **Valid Links**: Use full URLs (e.g., https://x.com/handle) - broken links hurt engagement
- **Frequency**: Update anytime - no limits
- **Track Fees**: Burned creation fees + swap burns = ecosystem value
